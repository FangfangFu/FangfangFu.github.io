Recipe 1

  1. Materials: Sticky rice, Reed leaf, Pork, Soy sauce, Sugar, Salt, Cooking wine, String
  2. Put sticky rice in warm water over one night
  3. Boil reed leaves for 30 minutes and leave them in hot water for 2-3 hours
  4. Mix pork with proper soy sauce, sugar, salt and cooking wine for 3 hours
  5. Put the sticky rice in reed leaves and wrap the leaves
  6. Put wrapped Zongzi in water and boil them 4 hours
